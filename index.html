Majors at IDN Boarding School

IDN Boarding School offers several majors for students to choose from. Here are the majors available at IDN Boarding School.

Visual Communication Design (VCD)

The VCD major covers design principles, visual communication, and multimedia. Graduates can work as Graphic Designers, UI/UX Designers, or Digital Marketing Specialists.

Software Engineering

The SE major covers software development. Graduates can work as Software Engineers. They can also pursue further studies or start their own tech companies.

Network Engineering

The NE major covers network administration, network security, and network architecture. Graduates can work as Network Administrators, Network Security Specialists, or Network Architects.
